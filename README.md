## вҡЎ WHAT IS CLUTCH

</div>

CLUTCH is an **agentic AI system** with **5 autonomous agents** powered by **Google Gemini 1.5 Flash**. When you're overwhelmed with deadlines, CLUTCH doesn't just remind you вҖ” it triages your tasks, builds step-by-step rescue plans, auto-schedules your calendar, checks in on you proactively, and even drafts extension emails when a deadline is impossible.

**Built for the crisis moment. Not the planning moment.**
